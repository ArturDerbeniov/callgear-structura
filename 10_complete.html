Common questions about Call Queing

What is a virtual call queue?
A call queue is a handy virtual PBX function that helps organize inbound calls into a queue to be routed to and answered by your available agents. To put it simply, managing calls on queue is how you avoid losing customers if none of your employees are free to attend to them at that moment.
